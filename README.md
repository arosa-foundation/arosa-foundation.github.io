# AROSA Foundation 🌐

Welcome to the AROSA Foundation! AROSA stands for **Active Research on Open Source AI**, and our mission is to make AI research freely accessible to everyone on this planet to improve the dignity and quality of life for all citizens.

## Introduction 🚀

AROSA is dedicated to creating, compiling, sharing, and developing new and better studies and research in the field of AI. By leveraging the power of open-source, we aim to foster an environment where AI research can flourish and contribute to the betterment of society.

## Mission and Vision 🌟

### Mission
To make AI research freely accessible, improving the dignity and quality of life for all citizens.

### Vision
To be a global leader in open-source AI research, fostering innovation, collaboration, and ethical practices to solve real-world problems.

## Core Programs and Initiatives 📊

### Research and Development Units (RDU)
Focus on launching and supporting various AI research projects.

### Educational Outreach and Community Engagement (EOCE)
Create educational resources, workshops, and community events to spread AI knowledge.

### Newbie Support Units (NSU)
Provide resources, mentorship, and support for newcomers to AI research.

### Research Advisory Board (RAB)
Provide guidance and expertise to ensure high-quality research and development.

### Operational Leadership Team (OLT)
Oversee the implementation of AROSA’s initiatives and daily operations.

### Ethics and Integrity Committee (EIC)
Ensure that all projects and research adhere to ethical guidelines and best practices.

## Transparency and Community Contributions 🌐

AROSA Foundation is committed to transparency and community involvement. We encourage users to contribute their own information, research, and ideas related to AI. 

If you have research, data, or any valuable information you’d like to share, please create an issue in this repository. Our team will review and integrate valuable contributions to ensure the most comprehensive and up-to-date AI research repository.

## Contributing 🤝

We welcome contributions from researchers, developers, educators, and anyone passionate about AI. To contribute, please create a issue. The members of CV will review the issues and will decide whether to add it to the offcial docs or not.

Thank you for your interest in AROSA. Together, we can create a brighter future through open-source AI research. 🌟
